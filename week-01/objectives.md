
# Week 1 Objectives

Primary Goal:
Establish baseline visibility into normal network behavior on a lab system.

Secondary Goal:
Perform a single controlled reconnaissance scan and analyze how it appears from a defensive perspective.

Success Criteria:
- Capture and review live network traffic
- Identify common protocols in use
- Observe outbound connections
- Perform one Nmap scan against a local VM
- Document observations and anomalies
